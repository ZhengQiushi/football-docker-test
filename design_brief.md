# Design Brief: Elite Gridiron Football Camp

This document outlines the visual identity and structural requirements for the Elite Gridiron Football Camp website. The goal is to create a high-energy, professional, and trustworthy online presence that appeals to both young athletes and their parents.

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette
The brand colors are designed to convey energy, professionalism, and the classic spirit of American football.
- **Deep Blue (#0A192F):** Used for primary backgrounds, headers, and navigation. Represents stability and authority.
- **Athletic Orange (#FF4D00):** Used for call-to-action buttons, highlights, and important accents. Represents energy and aggression.
- **White (#FFFFFF):** Used for primary text on dark backgrounds and clean secondary backgrounds.
- **Silver/Gray (#E2E8F0):** Used for subtle borders and secondary text elements.

### Typography
- **Headings:** Bold, all-caps sans-serif font (e.g., Inter or a custom varsity-style font) to evoke a sports broadcast feel.
- **Body Text:** Clean, highly readable sans-serif (e.g., Inter) for content-heavy sections.

### Imagery
- High-action, sharp photography of athletes in motion.
- Images of focused coaching and teamwork.
- Visual elements like turf textures, stadium lights, and dynamic diagonal lines.

## Key Website Sections

### 1. Hero Section
- **Visual:** High-impact background video or image of a football game/practice.
- **Headline:** Catchy, authoritative headline (e.g., "Elevate Your Game at the Premier Training Camp").
- **CTA:** "Register Now" or "Explore Programs" in Athletic Orange.

### 2. Programs Section
- Grid layout showcasing different camp tiers (e.g., Youth, High School, Elite).
- Each card includes a brief description, duration, and age group.

### 3. Coaches Section
- Profile photos of lead coaches with brief bios emphasizing their NFL or collegiate experience.
- Emphasize the "Pro-level" training aspect.

### 4. Stats & Impact
- Dynamic counters showing metrics like "500+ Athletes Trained," "50+ College Scholarships," or "15 Years of Excellence."

### 5. Contact & Location
- Embedded map of the training facility.
- Simple contact form for inquiries.
- Clear footer with social media links and camp dates.

## Technical Goals
- Fully responsive design (mobile-first).
- Fast loading times for media-heavy content.
- Interactive elements (hover states, smooth scrolling).
